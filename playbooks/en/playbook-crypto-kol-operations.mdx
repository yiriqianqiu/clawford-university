## Who It's For

## The Pain (Posting into the void)
- You tweet daily but nobody engages.
- You don't know what content resonates with crypto audiences.
- You have no system for tracking what works and doubling down.

---

## The Simple Learning Logic
- **Value first, followers second:** People follow accounts that make them smarter or richer. Pick one.
- **Consistency compounds:** One viral tweet is luck. Posting useful content daily for 90 days is a strategy.

---

## The Human‑Agent Split
**Agent does**
- Analyzes top‑performing crypto KOL accounts for content patterns (format, timing, topics).
- Generates content ideas based on trending on‑chain events and market narratives.
- Tracks engagement metrics and identifies which content types drive growth.

**Human does**
- Defines their unique angle and persona (alpha caller, educator, analyst, meme lord).
- Writes or refines the final content with authentic voice.
- Engages directly with followers and builds real relationships.

---

## The 4‑Step Routine (25 min/day)
**1) Trend Scan (5 min)**
Review the agent's daily brief: top trending tokens, on‑chain events, breaking news, and what top KOLs are talking about.

**2) Content Draft (10 min)**
The agent proposes 3 tweet ideas based on trends and your persona. Pick one, refine it, add your unique take.

**3) Engage (7 min)**
Reply to 5 relevant tweets from bigger accounts. The agent suggests high‑visibility threads to join. Add genuine insight, not "gm."

**4) Metrics Review (3 min)**
Check this week's numbers: impressions, engagement rate, follower growth, best‑performing tweet. The agent highlights what to repeat.

## Expected Outcomes (Time‑boxed)
- **7 days:** a defined persona and content calendar for the first month.
- **14 days:** 10+ published tweets with tracked engagement metrics.
- **30 days:** measurable follower growth and 2–3 tweets that outperformed your average by 3x.
