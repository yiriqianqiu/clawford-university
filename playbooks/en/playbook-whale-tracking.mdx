# Whale Tracking: Follow Smart Money On‑Chain
**Stop guessing what insiders know. Track what their wallets actually do.**

> **Disclaimer:** This playbook is for educational purposes only. Nothing here constitutes financial advice. On-chain signals are not guarantees of future price action. Always do your own research (DYOR).

---

## Quick Start
You hear about pumps after they happen. This playbook teaches you to spot whale accumulation and fund flows before the crowd catches on.

---

## Who It's For
On‑chain analysts and traders who want an edge by monitoring large wallet movements and interpreting smart money behavior.

---

## The Pain (Always late)
- By the time you see the pump, whales are already selling.
- You don't know which wallets to watch or what their moves mean.
- You confuse noise (random transfers) with signal (strategic accumulation).

---

## The Simple Learning Logic
- **Wallets are public diaries:** Every whale transaction tells a story—you just need to learn to read it.
- **Pattern over event:** A single transfer means nothing. Repeated behavior over days reveals intent.

---

## The Human‑Agent Split
**Agent does**
- Identifies whale wallets by on‑chain heuristics (balance size, transaction patterns, DEX interactions).
- Monitors target wallets for new transactions and token movements.
- Maps fund flows between wallets and flags cluster behavior.

**Human does**
- Curates the whale watchlist based on track record.
- Interprets the context behind movements (is this accumulation, distribution, or repositioning?).
- Decides whether to follow a signal or ignore it.

---

## The 4‑Step Routine (25 min/day)
**1) Alert Review (5 min)**
Check the agent's daily digest of whale wallet activity—new positions opened, large transfers, DEX swaps above threshold.

**2) Wallet Deep Dive (8 min)**
Pick one active whale. The agent maps their recent transactions: what they bought, when, and how much. Cross‑reference with token price movements.

**3) Signal Scoring (7 min)**
Rate the signal: Is this whale's history profitable? Are multiple whales converging on the same token? Is the timing consistent with accumulation patterns?

**4) Watchlist Update (5 min)**
Add or remove wallets from your watchlist. Note any emerging patterns for next week's analysis.

- **14 days:** 3+ instances where you identified whale accumulation before a price move.
- **30 days:** a repeatable smart money tracking system with proven signal quality.
